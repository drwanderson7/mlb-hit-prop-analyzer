🧮 Composite Score (0–100)
ComponentWeightNotes
Split xBA28%xBA vs RHP or vs LHP — confidence-weighted by split PA count (needs 80+ PA for full weight). Low-PA splits regress toward league average.
Pitcher matchup22%K% (38%) + xBA allowed (35%) + HH% allowed (15%) + whiff% (8%) + barrel% (4%). ERA excluded. Accounts for reverse-split pitchers.
Implied team runs14%Vegas-implied runs. Effect is order-dependent: slots 1-2 get 20% of effect (always get PAs), slots 3-5 get 55%, slots 6-9 get full effect.
Batting order10%PA volume by slot — leadoff ~4.7 PA/game, 9-hole ~3.6 PA/game.
Batter K%14%Strikeout = automatic out. Partially redundant with xBA but adds signal for extremes.
Hard Hit% + Barrel%10%Contact quality. Reduced — xBA already captures exit velo and launch angle.
Park factor3%Venue hit environment — reduced since xBA already captures exit velocity context.
Tiers
  • 🟢 Elite (85–100): Multiple factors aligning — strong single play
  • 🔵 Strong (70–84): Most factors favorable — good play
  • 🟡 Lean (55–69): Slight edge — parlay filler only
  • 🔴 Avoid (<55): Too many negatives — skip
❓ FAQ
When do lineups confirm? Usually 3–4 hours before first pitch ET. Run the tool again after they post to get confirmed orders.

Why does a star player score low? Could be a tough SP matchup, pitcher-friendly park, or high K% vs that handedness.

Are prop odds included? The tool doesn't pull live odds (would require a paid API key). Cross-reference your book for the line.

How current is Statcast data? Baseball Savant updates nightly — always reflects the most recent full season stats.
