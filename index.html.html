❓ FAQ
When do lineups confirm? Usually 3–4 hours before first pitch ET. Run the tool again after they post to get confirmed orders.

Why does a star player score low? Could be a tough SP matchup, pitcher-friendly park, or high K% vs that handedness.

Are prop odds included? The tool doesn't pull live odds (would require a paid API key). Cross-reference your book for the line.
